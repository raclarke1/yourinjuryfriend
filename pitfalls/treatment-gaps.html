Beware of Medical Treatment Gaps

A treatment gap is any significant lapse in time between injury and medical care, or between appointments during recovery.

Why Treatment Gaps Matter So Much - "If they were really hurt, they would've gone to the doctor." - "Something else must have happened." - "They made their injuries worse by failing to follow medical advice." - "They were already healed."

How to Avoid the Pitfall 1. Seek Medical Attention Immediately 2. Follow the Treatment Plan 3. Keep a Consistent Schedule 4. Document the Reasons for Any Gaps
